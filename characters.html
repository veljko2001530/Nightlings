When the lights go out, the Nightlings move as quiet as breath. They don’t come to scare you — they guard your dreams and push nightmares into the corner. If you hear a faint crackle or see a candle flicker by itself... that’s their sign.

Grimmie

Sits quietly by the door, listening to the hallway. When a bad shadow appears, Grimmie growls — and the shadow flees like smoke.

Nyx

The quietest of them all. Nyx erases traces of fear like chalk on a board. If you hear a whisper saying “it’s nothing,” that’s Nyx calming the dark.

Reddox

Always smiling, sparking a small flame in the dark. His laughter scatters bad dreams until they vanish like dust.

Brownie

Warm as a blanket, Brownie sits by the pillow and keeps watch. When your eyes grow heavy, he wiggles his nose and dreams turn soft.

Maybe there are more of them...
Can you collect them all?
